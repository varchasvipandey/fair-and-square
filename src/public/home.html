After producing music continuously for 3 days, Martin Garrix felt a strong headache. For the sake of pain relief, he decided to sleep. Suddenly he realized that he has entered into The World Of FL Studio. Every time he touches a fruit it disappears and adds on to his score. He is very confused now and has no other option but to continue whats going on. The fruits continue to respawn and after every respawn, FL Studio's rival Ableton attacks Martin Garrix. Help Martine Garrix to stay alive in his dream.

A Dragon Ball from an overrated anime came in this world from nowhere. This ball is yellow coloured and looks too damn creepy that dragons are afraid of getting close to it. The locals of the region have named this ball "Yellowish Junk".
The lords of the dragon world are fighting each other to protect their town from this Yellowish Junk. A town can take only two hits of this junk before getting destroyed completely.
This is not a joke, the fight is on!

In The World Of FL Studio :: Instruction

Genre: Platformer

Objective: Survival


Collect all the fruits which are infinite in number. These fruits are FL Studio mango, which can't be eaten.
The game starts with 18 fruits which can be collected easily without getting into trouble. Each fruit collected will give Martin 10 points.

After every collection of 18 fruits, a bomb will be spawned. This will not explode but will bounce all over the game screen.
This bomb is Ableton, avoid colliding with it.

That is not it, Ableton will be spawned after every 18th fruit collected. So Martin may end up between a bunch of Ableton.

Try beating the developers high-score because playing this will not bring Martin out from his dream.
But don't you worry, don't you worry child
See heaven's got a plan for you!

Yes, Swedish House Mafia is on a rescue mission. But the sad news is, he is rescuing the child not Martin. So survive as long as you can.

Dragon Pong :: Instruction

Genre: Multiplayer-Sports

Objective: Protect Area


This is a multiplayer only ping-pong game. Each player has to protect the wall behind the board. These boards are lords of their world. No one cares from where they are because everyone wants to win.

Player-1 CONTROLS:
W: Move up
S: Move down

Player-2 CONTROLS:
⇑: Move up
⇓: Move down

The player who fails to protect its area for 2 Times will be declared as the loser.
